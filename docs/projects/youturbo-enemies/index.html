YouTurbo enemies

Some of the enemies made for the mobile video game YouTurbo, developed by HeYou Games. They were created in Photoshop, exported in pieces to Unity 5, where they were mounted and animated.

More than 900 enemies were created for the game, an average of 30 enemies per artist per week.
