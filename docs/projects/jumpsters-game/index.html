Jumpsters Game

Characters, enemies and art made for the mobile game Jumpsters developed by HeYou Games.

The assets were first made in Adobe Illustrator, then retouched in Adobe Photoshop, exported to 3D meshes in Autodesk 3ds Max, and finally exported to Unity.

I made all the art and processes, from concept to final implementation.
